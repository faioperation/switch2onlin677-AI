From its beginnings in Baghdad, DBC has grown into
a nationwide powerhouse, representing international
brands and creating premium retail experiences that
redefine luxury for the Iraqi consumer. Today, we
stand as the partner of choice for global brands
seeking to enter and thrive in the Iraqi market.
Founded in 1998, Dhifaf Baghdad Cosmetics Center
(DBC) has established itself as Iraq’s leading
distributor and retailer of luxury perfumes, cosmetics,
skincare, haircare, and fashion.
Proudly owned and operated by Iraqi entrepreneurs,
DBC has been a trusted partner for over 25 years,
building a reputation for exclusivity, integrity, and
excellence.

Our Purpose & Commitment
At DBC, our purpose is simple yet powerful: to bring global luxury closer to Iraq. We
are committed to ensuring every brand we represent receives not only a platform
but also the respect, attention, and tailored strategies needed to succeed in a
unique and fast-growing market.
Our commitment extends beyond distribution, we focus on building strong brand
identities in Iraq, nurturing consumer trust, and delivering sustainable growth for
every partner.

DBC has built long-term collaborations with over 420
international brands, carefully selected to bring global
luxury in perfumes, cosmetics, skincare, haircare, and
fashion to the Iraqi market.
Milestones & Achievements
Market Leadership
With more than 25 years of expertise, DBC has become
Iraq’s most trusted distributor and retailer of luxury
beauty, consistently setting benchmarks for quality,
exclusivity, and consumer trust.
Nationwide Coverage
Through six regional offices and full distribution across all 18
provinces, DBC ensures seamless market reach, with plans
to expand to eight offices by 2026.
Successful Launches & Growth
DBC has successfully introduced and expanded the
presence of global icons elevating their position in the Iraqi
market through exclusive launches and tailored campaigns.
Retail & Distribution Excellence
Our expertise in premium retail environments and robust
logistics ensures that every brand is represented with
international standards, from luxury counters to flagship
boutiques.
Brand-Building Events & Activations
DBC has hosted and managed premium events, in-store
activations, and nationwide campaigns that strengthen
brand awareness, enhance consumer loyalty, and create
memorable luxury experiences.
