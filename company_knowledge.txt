Dhifaf Baghdad Cosmetics Center (DBC) - Company Knowledge Base

ABOUT DBC
Dhifaf Baghdad Cosmetics Center (DBC) was founded in 1998 and has established itself as Iraq’s leading distributor and retailer of luxury perfumes, cosmetics, skincare, haircare, and fashion.

DBC is proudly owned and operated by Iraqi entrepreneurs and has built a trusted reputation for exclusivity, integrity, excellence, and long-term partnerships.

From its beginnings in Baghdad, DBC has expanded into a nationwide powerhouse, representing international luxury brands and creating premium retail experiences across Iraq.

DBC is considered a trusted partner for global brands seeking successful entry and growth in the Iraqi market.
